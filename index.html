About Me


Hello! Welcome to my page that’s all about me. My name is Mariam Ali, but a lot of people call me Muna, so feel free to do so. I also like to go on road trips, my friends and I go to Chicago like every few months since it’s so close. I’m trying to be craftier and tried painting and crocheting but that was honestly hard haha. But enjoy my page!
